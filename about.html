Our Story

At a Glance

It All Started

In London In 1986

It was only Fish that was prepared by Food Loung: Fresh, Crisp and served with Esteem. Born in London in 1986 by couple duo Serena and Brian Le Pedre, Food Loung received its first Michelin star in 2000 and two more in 2004 & 2006. It was those days when Le Pedre’s set to evolve Food Loung in Manhattan in 2010 .

Le Pedre has become one of the world’s famous chefs and his expertise extended far above the kitchen. After unexpected passing of Serena Le Pedre, her disciple and a supporter Chef Jean Daniel took an initiative with a simple ideology that Fish is the star of the platter, and rolled preparing the freshest fish menu.

The Story

Continues Today In NYC

Within 4 months of Food Loung’s entry in New York, the restaurant earned its prosperous 5 stars rating and was on the top of the rope with the same ranking for 6 reviews.

It was during this time, Le Pedre has travelled the corners of the world and laid a path to Food Loung, which has then evolved to be a contemporary, international and elegant restaurant through their now world renowned chefs Oliva Lerou and Travis Lau. This duo has developed exotic flavors and textures from broths & vegetable juices and played them with the East’s aromatics flavors discovering an emerging menu of meat, vegetable and desserts.
